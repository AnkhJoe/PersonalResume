Project Details 2

Project Background: The concept of a business platform was proposed to allow direct communication between the client and the producer for personalized and customized needs, as well as to provide a clientele of skilled technicians with specialties.

Development Platform: WeChat Small Program

Innovation: The project team designed a C2C model business platform that allows clients to communicate directly with producers to meet personalized and customized needs. The platform provides an environment that allows technicians with niche specialties to showcase their work, while clients can select service providers based on their personal preferences, enabling personalization and customization of services.
